🚆 Getting Here

Plan your trip to Royal Randwick. Live travel updates on race day.

LIVE
L2 light rail running to schedule. Allow extra time leaving after the last race.
🚊 Light rail · easiest
L2 Randwick line to Royal Randwick or Wansey Rd stop — a short walk to the gates. Runs from Circular Quay via Central.
🚆 Train + connect
Take any T-line to Central, then the L2 light rail or a bus to the course.
🚌 Bus
Routes along Alison Rd & Anzac Parade (339, 372, 374, 376, 377) stop close to the entrances.
🅿️ Driving & parking
Limited on-course parking via the Alison Rd gates. Metered street parking in Kensington & Randwick fills early — allow extra time.
🚕 Rideshare drop-off
Set your destination to Royal Randwick, Alison Rd. Pick-ups are easiest from Wansey Rd after the last.
♿ Accessible access
Step-free entry at Gate 1 (Member Lawn). Accessible parking available on request via the venue team.
⏱️ Beat the queues
Add your ticket to Apple Wallet before you arrive and enter via your allocated gate. Gates open 2 hours before the first race.
